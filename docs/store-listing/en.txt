PlainTab is a beautiful, fast, and quiet new tab page.

Why? Because your new tab should first give you a comfortable wallpaper, not ads, news, trending feeds, or a screen full of shortcuts.

PlainTab does one simple thing: open a new tab, show your wallpaper, then let you search, type a URL, or open your own shortcuts. It does not fight for your attention or turn your browser homepage into another content feed.

Why choose PlainTab?

【A Picture Right Away】
• Extremely fast local wallpaper loading — PlainTab stores a lightweight preview snapshot locally and loads it instantly every time you open a new tab, reducing blank-screen flicker.
• High-resolution wallpapers switch smoothly in the background, while the previous image remains underneath as a fallback, so you do not need to wait for the network before seeing a background.
• Bing daily wallpapers support caching and updates; after you have used PlainTab once, later opens feel smoother.

【Wallpaper Freedom】
• Supports Bing daily wallpapers and your own uploaded images.
• Supports selecting a local wallpaper folder, ideal if you already keep a wallpaper collection.
• Supports RSS image sources and custom APIs, simple when you want it simple, flexible when you want to tinker.
• Local wallpapers support up to 12 rotating images with a 3×4 gallery preview, so you can delete or replace them anytime.

【Quiet, Not Bare】
• The homepage stays restrained: wallpaper, search bar, and a few necessary controls, without filling the page with widgets.
• The search bar supports adjustable position, size, corner radius, opacity, and visibility.
• The search bar can stay always visible, appear only when needed, or stay hidden.
• Shortcuts are managed in the command palette, with search, add, edit, and bookmark import support, so they do not cover the wallpaper.
• Hidden space lets you keep links you do not want to display directly, keeping the homepage clean.

【Privacy First】
• No ads, no tracking, and no browsing-data collection.
• Locally uploaded wallpapers are stored in your browser's local storage. They are not uploaded, synced, or routed through third-party servers.
• Search and shortcut settings are designed for local use. Your new tab should belong to you.

【Lightweight And Transparent】
• Built with vanilla JavaScript, CSS, and browser APIs, with no frontend framework, no build step, and no large runtime dependency.
• Works as a Chrome / Edge new tab extension, and can also run directly as a web page.
• Fully open source, suitable for everyday use and for learning browser extension development, frontend projects, and AI-assisted engineering.

PlainTab is for you if you want:

• A better-looking new tab without information-feed noise
• Daily wallpapers, or a place to manage your own wallpaper collection
• Search, shortcuts, bookmark import, and backup features without homepage clutter
• A homepage that is not covered by dozens of shortcut tiles
• A lightweight, transparent extension without complex build tooling
• A real, complete browser extension project to learn from

What PlainTab will not do:

• No news feeds, trending lists, or recommended content
• No ads, sponsored cards, or promotional slots
• No account system, social features, or cloud content stream
• No wall of shortcut tiles covering the homepage
• No autoplay video or animated backgrounds
